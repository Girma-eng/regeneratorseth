The Problem We Are Solving

Ethiopia's agricultural sector faces a dual crisis that our RegeneratorsTech Suite directly addresses:

  • Mechanization Access Gap: Over 85% of smallholders lack reliable access to affordable machinery, relying on inefficient broker systems that charge 200-300% premiums.
  • Digital Disconnect: Farm management remains manual and fragmented, with no integrated digital tools for planning, tracking, or analytics.
  • Asset Inefficiency: Commercial farms suffer from 40-60% machinery idle time with no digital management tools.
  • Market Fragmentation: Farmers lack transparent access to services, inputs, and fair markets.

This perfect storm of challenges keeps productivity 30-50% below potential while threatening national food security goals.
