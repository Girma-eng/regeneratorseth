Benefits

Discover how we're benefiting in transforming Ethiopian agriculture through integrated technology and farmer-centric solutions

Improved Operational Efficiency

The app enables farmers to schedule, assign, and track all farming tasks—from land preparation to harvesting—using real-time data. This reduces time wastage, minimizes human error, and ensures that machinery, labor, and inputs are optimally utilized.

25% less downtime 18% cost savings

Data-Driven Decision Making

Comprehensive data collection and reporting allow users to analyze trends in fuel consumption, labor use, and machine performance. This promotes informed decision-making for both short-term management and long-term strategic planning.

30% more accurate planning 15% input use reduction

Real-Time Field Insights

Make fact-based decisions based on real-time insights into weather conditions, pest risk and crop health by field. Analyze satellite data and operations management data by field.

25% less downtime 18% cost savings

Enhanced Decision Support and Planning

The app features analytical tools for tractor–implement matching, economically optimal tractor size selection, replacement strategy, and break-even analysis. These modules enable users to identify the most efficient machinery combinations, reduce equipment costs, and make evidence-based investment decisions.

20% higher equipment utilization 12% lower maintenance cost

Cost Optimization and Financial Transparency

Integrated modules calculate production costs per crop, field, or operation, distinguishing between fixed and variable costs. The inclusion of break-even analysis helps farmers determine profitability thresholds and make sound marketing and budgeting decisions.

28% improved cost tracking 20% higher profit margin

Sustainable Resource Utilization

By monitoring machine hours, fuel, and input usage, the app supports efficient resource management, reduces waste, and promotes environmentally friendly practices aligned with sustainable agricultural development goals.

18% fuel savings 25% lower carbon footprint

Strengthened Accountability and Record Keeping

The app ensures accurate recording of all field activities, equipment usage, and operator performance, improving transparency and traceability across all farm operations.

100% traceability 40% faster report generation

Contribution to Digital and Smart Agriculture

By combining monitoring, analytics, and decision support features, the app accelerates the adoption of precision agriculture and digital transformation in farming systems—laying the groundwork for smarter, data-driven, and sustainable food production.

35% faster digital adoption 50+ farmers onboarded
